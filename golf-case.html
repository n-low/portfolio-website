    • Mobile App For Beginner Golfers

    Overview

    Client/Company: Ximity Labs

    Duration: April 2023 — Present

    Tools Used: Figma, various UX research methods.

    Role: UX Researcher/Designer

    Introduction

    NewToGolf is a mobile application tailored for beginner golfers, aiming to simplify the learning process and enhance their overall golfing experience. The app integrates educational resources, detailed course maps, and GPS-based instructions to provide comprehensive support to new players. This case study outlines the development process, key features, user benefits, and includes personas to illustrate the app's impact.

    Problem Statement

    Beginners often struggle with understanding golf rules, navigating courses, and executing proper techniques. NewToGolf addresses these challenges by offering a user-friendly platform with interactive learning tools, detailed maps, and real-time guidance.

    Objectives

    • Educate Beginners. Simplify golf rules and etiquette learning.
    • Facilitate Course Navigation. Simplify golf rules and etiquette learning.
    • Enhance Skill Development. Simplify golf rules and etiquette learning.

    Features

    1. Learning the Rules

    • Interactive Rulebook: Engaging quizzes and videos explaining golf rules.
    • Etiquette Tips: Guidelines on proper golf course behavior.

    2. Golf Course Maps

    • High-Resolution Maps: Clear and detailed maps of golf courses.
    • GPS Integration: Real-time location tracking and hole navigation.

    3. GPS-Based Instructions

    • Hole-by-Hole Guidance: Detailed instructions for each hole.
    • Visual Aids: Diagrams and videos demonstrating techniques for different shots.

    4. User-Friendly Interface

    • Intuitive Design: Simplified navigation and user experience.
    • Customization Options: Personalization based on user preferences.

    5. Community Features

    • Forums and Chat: Platform for users to connect and share experiences.
    • Event Listings: Information on local golf events and beginner-friendly tournaments.

    Development Process

    1. Research and Planning

    • Surveys and interviews with beginner golfers to identify key challenges.
    • Collaboration with golf instructors for expert insights.

    2. Design and Prototyping

    • Creation of wireframes and prototypes focusing on ease of use.
    • User testing with beginner golfers to refine features and interface.

    3. Development

    • Agile development methodology for iterative progress.
    • Integration of GPS and mapping technologies for accurate navigation.

    4. Testing and Launch

    • Extensive testing across multiple devices to ensure functionality.
    • Beta release for user feedback and final adjustments.
